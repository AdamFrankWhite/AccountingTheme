Welcome to Accounting Solutions Accountants, Manchester

We are a firm of Qualified (ACCA) Accountants in Manchester with over 15 years of experience and offer a ‘Fixed Fee‘ service. We specialise in helping new and existing firms and by utilising our expert tax knowledge we suggest ways to help you Pay Less Tax and Increase your Profits.

As tax specialists we ensure that our clients only pay the absolute minimum of tax – all too often we have taken on a new client and find that they have been missing out on the most basic tax-saving opportunities.

Our Services

Personal Tax Returns

Let Accounting Solutions Accountants in Manchester prepare your Personal Tax Return and ensure that you pay the absolute minimum amount possible.

Year-End Accounts

We provide an efficient, reliable and affordable year-end accountancy service. This includes the accounts preparation, discussion at the client meeting and subsequent filing of the accounts with both Companies House and HMRC.

Management Accounts

We can prepare regular management accounts to help you assess the performance of your business, for example, assess your customer account balances, review your supplier account balances, identify bad debts and surpluses or deficiencies in resources.

Bookkeeping

We can complete the bookkeeping on your behalf on a monthly or quarterly basis. Once we have completed your bookkeeping we will return your paperwork.

VAT Services and Payroll

We can assist with VAT registration; advise on VAT planning and administration; provide VAT reconciliation and checking; complete VAT returns; file VAT returns online; negotiate with HMRC in disputes, including representation; and more.

Company Formation

Here at Accounting Solutions, we have helped many new and existing clients, to successfully set up and start their own limited company. We can help limit your liabilities and gain a tax advantage, resulting in less tax being payable.
